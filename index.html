ABOUT THE EVENT

Open innovation is one of the founding principles of the event. Through this methodology, everyone can contribute their skills and expertise to the development of innovative solutions. With this in mind, the IEEE ENSI Student Brunch, in collaboration with its CIS chapter, will offer the AI Hackathon 3.0, which we are very excited about.
The objective of this hackathon is to open up exchanges, collaborations, and the application of technological knowledge in neuroscience. Please note that the third edition will take place on July 2 and 3 in an online format. AI HACKATHON
